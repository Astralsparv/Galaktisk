You don't have to code your own site! Let us do the heavy lifting for you. Use this website to generate a Galaktisk page in the theme of MySpace generated with your information

How do I host my site?

We suggest using neocities.org, but you can use any host you'd like.

For neocities, go to neocities.org and make an account.
Once you've created an account, go to neocities.org/dashboard, this will be the root of your webpage.

What do I need to put in my root? (applies to all hosts)

Place a square page picture named icon.png in the root of your page - this will be used for your page if you used the page builder & it will be used for the Galaktisk browse page

If you used the page builder, place the index.html file that was downloaded when you generated your code into the root of your page

How can I get this on the Galaktisk browser?

As of now, fill out the google form (press the Join button at the top) with your name, link and a form of contact (e.g: email, discord username) and your site will be posted when it's seen.
