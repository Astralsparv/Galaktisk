Build your Blog

This builds an index.html for a blog page. Remember to add this to your blogs.json for it to appear on your blogs page (blogs page can be created alongside your main page)!


General




How to format this section

Hi! As this part of the page is formatted differently, you will have to seperate your input with formatters.
Use | to put your data in

e.g:
tag1|tag2|tag3

programming|project|complete

You can add as many tags or as little tags as you like (minimum one tag)!


To go from here, create a folder in your /blog/ page. It's best to name it similarly to your title or in a numbered fashion, e.g: blog1, blog2.
Place this index.html in that newly created folder.

At this time, there is no editor for blogs.json - see pagebuilder for more information.
