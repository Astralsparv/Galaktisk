How to format this section

Hi! As this part of the page is formatted differently, you will have to seperate your input with formatters.
Use | to put your data in and a new line to seperate sections

e.g:
name|details|what to link name|details|what to link

Email|coolsillystuffonly100@gmail.com|mailto:coolsillystuffonly100@gmail.com
SpaceHey|@astralsparv|https://spacehey.com/astralsparv
